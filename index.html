Move Player From To

Game Summary

Board & Setup:

  • Played on an 8x4 grid.
  • Player A's pieces start at the bottom; Player B's at the top.

Objective:

  • Create a connected chain from your start to your opponent's start.

Moves:

  • Move: Slide a piece to an adjacent empty cell.
  • Swap: Exchange with an adjacent opponent's piece. A swapped piece cannot be swapped again until a normal move is taken by either player

Win Condition:

  • Win by forming a contiguous connection between your start and opponent's start.
